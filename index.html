01 — About

Data Strategist.
Builder. Nonconformist.

I'm Mayra, I ask the right questions, find the answers that matter, and build solutions that actually move the needle. A decade working with multinationals like Merck, Boehringer Ingelheim, Pfizer and Air France-KLM taught me that the real problem is rarely the data itself it's the gap between data and decision.

That conviction led me to co-found Synthesis Data alongside my partner and a talented team a consultancy built on one belief: data without strategy is noise. We help businesses of all sizes, from global corporations to growing SMEs, cut through that noise and turn their data into clear, agile, and confident decisions.

Currently contracting independently for a global pharmaceutical company, supporting Data Governance strategy across international operations, while pursuing an MBA at EUDE Business School in Madrid. If you believe data without strategy is noise or want to make sure your data is working harder for your strategy, let's talk.
